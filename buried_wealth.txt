===ENGLISH===
Once upon a time there lived a farmer with four lazy sons.
The farmer himself worked very hard in his fields, very day, from dawn to dusk.
---PORTION---
As a result of his hard work, every few months, he would reap a rich harvest.
One day, the farmer fell very ill.
---PORTION---
The best doctors were called in, but one by one, they gave up all hope.
The farmer was very worried.
---PORTION---
Lying in his bed, he thought, ' All my four sons are very lazy '.
' Who will look after my fields after my death? '.
---PORTION---
' Who will do all the hard work of tilling the fields, sowing the seeds and bringing in the harvest? '.
' If my sons do not do all this, how will they earn and who will take care of the family? '.
---PORTION---
He thought long and hard and slowly an idea started forming in his head.
He called his four sons to his bedside and said in whisper, ' I have buried a lot of wealth in different spots in my field '.
---PORTION---
' After my death, you must all dig up the field and locate the buried treasure '.
Soon thereafter, the farmer breathed his last.
---PORTION---
Barely had they finished cremating their Father than the sons rushed to the field and began to dig all over the place.
They dug here and they dug there, over many days, but not a single coin was to be found.
---PORTION---
The four sons were very angry.
' Our Father lied to us ', they grumbled.
---PORTION---
' We toiled day after day, but our efforts have yielded nothing! ' they said angrily.
Their Mother, who had overheard them speaking in this manner said, ' The rains will start soon '.
---PORTION---
' Since you have already dug up the fields, why not sow seeds for the next crop? '.
Since they had not found any treasure and the fields had already been dug up, the sons followed the advice of the Mother The rains were good that year and soon the fields were full of tall, swaying crops.
---PORTION---
A few months later, the four sons were able to bring in a rich harvest.
They took their produce to the market in the nearby town and were able to sell all they had brought with them for a tidy sum.
---PORTION---
Looking at the money they had made, they were delighted!
They rushed back home to their Mother to tell her how much they had been able to earn.
---PORTION---
Their Mother heard them out and said, ' Your Father had told you there was a lot of wealth buried in the field '.
' Today, you have been able to get the treasure he had spoken of to you '.
---PORTION---
The four sons looked at each other and said, 'We have learnt our lesson, Mother'.
' From now on, we will work hard every day in our fields and make sure, year after year, we bring in the wealth that our Father had left buried in the fields '.
