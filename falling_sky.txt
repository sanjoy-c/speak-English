===ENGLISH===
Once there lived a rather timid rabbit in a jungle who scared easily.
It would get alarmed even at any sound or the sight of anything unusual.
---PORTION---
It could never sleep peacefully and would keep its ears pricked up to catch the slightest sound.
One summer afternoon, it was sitting under a mango tree, thinking of taking a nap.
---PORTION---
Suddenly, a large ripe mango fell from high above on to the ground next to the rabbit, making a big sound on contact with the earth.
The startled and scared rabbit began to run, shouting while it did so, ' Run for your lives, for the sky is falling down '.
---PORTION---
A deer grazing in the grounds close by, seeing the rabbit running like the wind and shouting at the opt of its voice, got alarmed.
It too started running for its life, shouting along with the rabbit, ' Run, for the sky is falling down '.
---PORTION---
Seeing the two of them running in this manner, soon a fox, a giraffe and a zebra also joined them.
Soon, there was whole herd of animals running hard, in no particular direction, all shouting together, ' Run, the sky is falling down '.
---PORTION---
A lion heard this noise and decided to do something about it.
It got up and drawing himself to his full height let out a loud roar.
---PORTION---
Hearing the roar, all the panicking animals immediately came to a halt.
The lion walked over to the herd of animals and demanded to know why they were all running in this manner, shouting at the top of their voices.
---PORTION---
They all said to him together, ' Run for your life, for the sky is falling down '.
' Which one of you has seen this piece of sky falling down? ' asked the lion.
---PORTION---
The animals all looked at each other, not knowing what to say.
Finally, the rabbit stepped forward and, shaking with fear, spoke of the loud sound and the ground shaking under the mango tree.
---PORTION---
' Take me to the mango tree and we shall see about this story of the sky falling down ', said the lion.
So all the animals, led by the lion with the rabbit by its side, trooped back to the mango tree There they searched all around the tree and finally found only a big, ripe mango lying on the ground.
---PORTION---
The lion pointed to the mango and asked the rabbit, ' Is this the portion of the sky over which you raised such an alarm? '.
The rabbit stood there hanging its head in shame and so did all the other animals.
---PORTION---
Slowly, they turned around and walked away sheepishly.
Moral of the story : Find out facts for yourself before blindly following the herd.
